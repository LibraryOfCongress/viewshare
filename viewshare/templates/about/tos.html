ViewShare.org End-User Terms of Service

Welcome to ViewShare.org
ViewShare is a free, open source platform for generating and customizing views (interactive maps, timelines, facets, tag clouds) that allow users to experience digital collections in new ways. With simple spreadsheets and other structured data types, collections are made more visual, usable and sharable with the ViewShare software.
